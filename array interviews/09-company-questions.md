## 2024–2025 Trends

- **Interval problems exploding**: Meta's `Merge Intervals` (110 freq) is the highest raw number in array category. Every company tests it. Know insert and non-overlapping variants too.
- **Sliding window is a must**: `Sliding Window Maximum` (monotonic deque) differentiates candidates — Amazon (32), Google (8), Microsoft (6).
- **In-place tricks**: Microsoft specifically asks `First Missing Positive` (16) and `Set Matrix Zeroes` (8) more than others.
- **Greedy array**: `Jump Game` and `Gas Station` consistently appear at Amazon and Microsoft.
- **Two pointers with dedup**: `3Sum` and `3Sum Closest` appear at all companies — practice the dedup pattern.
